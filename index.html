With a design reminiscent of a 1920s transatlantic cruise liner, award-winning food and drink outlets and an electric energy, Sea Containers London is the remarkable South Bank hotel. It’s a destination where glamour meets brutalism – and it's your London anchor on the River Thames.

OUR ROOMS

A space to unwind, recharge and regroup, each of our guest rooms are the ideal South Bank base from which to explore London.Whether you opt for one of our standard rooms, or book into an impressive river view apartment suite, you’re guaranteed to have a memorable evening.Award-winning designer Tom Dixon

has turned his hand to all aspects of the rooms, perfecting every detail from the artwork to the positioning of the beds. Featuring bespoke furniture, custom-designed dimmable lighting, and impressive marble bathrooms, our guest rooms are here to be your London home.
